About Us

We are modern and a state of art blood bank located in the heart of Pimpri-Chinchwad city. We are a NGO and licensed to provide blood and its components like Red Cells, R.D.P.,S.D.P., F.F.P etc. to the needy patients. We are providing the door step delivery of blood and its components to 80-100 hospitals in and around Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad area with full commitment and utmost urgency. Since demand is always very high and supply low, we are unable to meet both ends i.e. Demand and Supply. It can be considered in the present background of increased number of vehicular accidents, more planned surgeries, increased heart bypass and cancer surgeries that demand outweighs the supply. we will ensure the following privileges for the donors.
1. Issue of appreciation certificate on the same day.
2. Universal colored blood donor card cum coupon.( stating Blood group
3. Organizer credit 10% free units to the cases recommended by organizers
4. Blood donor himself will get free blood during his life period.
5. We provide blood free of cost to Thalasemia and Haemophilia patients.
Please note that blood donation is voluntary social activity pertaining to the service of humanity. So be a part of this noble mission i.e. SERVICE TO HUMANITY.
